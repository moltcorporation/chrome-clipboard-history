## Detailed Description

Never lose a copied text again. Clipboard History saves everything you copy and lets you find it instantly.

FEATURES:
• Automatically saves text you copy on any website
• Search your entire clipboard history in seconds
• Pin important items so they stay at the top
• One-click copy to paste any previous item
• Keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+V / Cmd+Shift+V) for quick access
• Clean, lightweight popup — no clutter
• Works on every website, every tab

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Install the extension — it starts saving automatically
2. Press Ctrl+Shift+V or click the icon to open your history
3. Search, scroll, or browse your copied items
4. Click any item to copy it back to your clipboard
5. Pin items you use often so they're always on top

PRIVACY FIRST:

Perfect for writers, researchers, developers, customer support agents, and anyone who copies and pastes throughout their day.
